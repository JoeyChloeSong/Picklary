# PickleLevel enrichment notes

This version adds visual and editorial enrichment for the paddle and professional player sections.

## Added

- 24 paddle records now include a USD reference price, price/source note, official product/source URL, richer summary text, and editorial power/control/spin/forgiveness/speed scores.
- 24 locally generated SVG paddle product-card illustrations were added under `assets/img/paddles/`.
- 12 professional player records now include biography text, hometown/base, age/height when available, turned-pro/hand/residence fields, official profile links, secondary profile links, and richer skill/achievement notes.
- 12 locally generated SVG player profile-card illustrations were added under `assets/img/players/`.
- Paddle listing, paddle detail pages, player listing, and player detail pages now render images, prices, biographies, source buttons, and richer specification cards.

## Important image note

The player profile cards are original illustrated placeholders, not real photographic likenesses. Real pro-player photographs and official product photographs should only be embedded after confirming license/permission from the athlete, tour, photographer, or brand. The pages include official profile/source buttons so visitors can view current official photos and stats from the source.

## Price and profile note

Prices are USD reference prices from official or source pages and can change with promotions, region, inventory, and model variants. Rankings, DUPR ratings, sponsors, and player equipment also change. Verify source links before publishing buying recommendations or current-rank claims.

## DUPR level selector fix

- The home-page DUPR rail now links every tick directly to its matching level page: 2.0, 2.5, 3.0, 3.5, 4.0, 4.5, and 5.0.
- A visible "DUPR level quick select" button row was added under the home-page DUPR rail, so visitors can choose any level without scrolling.
- The Korean home page now shows: "DUPR 레벨 바로 선택" with all seven level buttons.
- The existing level-card grid and left-side level bar remain unchanged.
