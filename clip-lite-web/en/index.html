Choose the best way to run it

The video and audio codecs inside the file matter more than the file extension.

Web version recommended

Fastest when you already know the IN and OUT points

  • When you already know the IN and OUT times and want to enter them directly
  • MP4 · H.264/AVC video + AAC audio
  • WebM · VP8/VP9 video + Opus audio
  • Standard Full HD or short 4K videos
  • Videos that play correctly with picture and sound in your browser
Direct time entry · MP4 or ZIP export · no server upload
Windows local version recommended

When the local app is a better fit

  • HEVC/H.265, 10-bit or HDR video
  • Large or long videos of about 1 GB or more
  • Videos that are demanding for a browser, such as 4K/60fps
  • Videos that play audio only or show a black screen on the web

Free online video cutter for pickleball rallies and everyday clips

Picklary Clip Lite helps players, coaches, families, and creators cut the useful moments from a long video without installing a full editing suite. Your source video stays on your device while you mark IN and OUT points and export one combined video, separate clips, or both.

Built for rally review

Move backward or forward by 1, 3, or 5 seconds, mark each rally, and keep the selected scenes connected to the video timeline.

No server upload

Processing happens in your browser. This reduces upload waiting time and keeps personal match footage on your own device.

Free to use

Use the browser version for quick clipping, then share the selected rallies for feedback or review. Return to the Picklary home page for level guides and pickleball tools.
