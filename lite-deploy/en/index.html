What changed in v0.7.5

Controls stay visible

DualCam previews now use QVideoSink canvases, preventing native video surfaces from covering play controls and timelines.

Scrollable DualCam page

Auto-sync, output settings, and composition controls remain reachable on smaller and high-DPI displays.

Output-position labels

Video 1 is clearly identified as Left/Top and Video 2 as Right/Bottom in the output.

Audio auto-sync

Find a shared sound section, calculate the recording offset, and review the suggested start points before applying.

4K Horizontal defaults

DualCam opens with Horizontal layout and 4K quality selected.

GPU-first processing

NVIDIA NVENC is tested before use, with automatic CPU fallback when needed.
